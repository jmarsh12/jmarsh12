Target Audience

New Age Weather is geared toward everyone, old and young. If you exist and you need weather information, New Age Weather is for you. It's even better if you enjoy the great outdoors! We provide the best information that is location specific just for you.

Personas

John Doe

John is an avid bird watcher. He loves to go outdoors and find the most exotic birds in the area. Instrumental to John is reliable weather information. If he looks up the weather and it says it will be sunny, but it's rainy, there won't be any birds out for him to see. He needs to know which days and times are best to go out.

Jorge Tortilla

Jorge is a licensed drone pilot. He constantly needs to know what the weather is so he can know if his drone can handle the weather. In order to fly a drone, Jorge needs weather that is not windy, not too hot or too cold, and not wet. Drones can be very finicky when it comes to weather, so it's crucial for him to know the best weather info around.

Scenarios

  1. What's the high today?
  2. I feel like going on a hike. What's the weather like in the mountains?
  3. What's the wind speed?
  4. When can I go out and fly my drone?
  5. Pool weather?
